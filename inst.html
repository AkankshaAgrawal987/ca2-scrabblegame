INSTRUCTIONS

  • Enter both player's names and click "START" to begin!
  • Place tiles on the board to spell out words. Click on the letter on your rack and then click again on the location you'd like to place it.
  • Except for the first turn, at least one of your letters must touch a letter already in play, and your new letters must all be in the same row or column, and be touching another tile.
  • At the beginning of your turn, click "SHOW TILES" to reveal the letters on your rack.
  • After you click "Submit" your score is tallied and your rack refreshed from the tile bag.
  • Clicking "Refresh" will give you a brand new rack and shuffle your current tiles back into the bag. Doing this forfeits your current turn.
  • Clicking "Exit" will take you to the gameover page where you will see the winner of the game.
  • To again start the game click on "Restart" which will show after the end of the game
  • Clicking "Exit" will take you to the main page.
